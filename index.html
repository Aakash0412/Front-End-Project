About SP Engineering

SP Engineering is a trusted name in the installation and assembly of industrial elevators. With a team of experienced professionals and a commitment to quality and safety, we ensure every project is executed with precision and in compliance with top industry standards.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading force in the industrial elevator and crane sector, empowering industries with reliable, innovative, and sustainable vertical mobility and lifting solutions.

Our Mission

To build SP Engineering as a standard provider of electrical services, specializing in the installation and maintenance of FLP lifts, EOT cranes, and hoists—delivering reliable solutions across oil & gas, petrochemical, and power sectors in full compliance with international standards.

Our Core Services

Installation & Commissioning

Expert erection, testing, and commissioning of industrial lifts and EOT cranes, ensuring safe and compliant deployment.

Maintenance Services

Comprehensive maintenance including Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), Preventive and Breakdown services for lifts, cranes, and electrical equipment.

Supply & Skilled Workforce

Supply of electrical components and deployment of trained manpower, including certified lift/EOT operators and technical staff.
